The story
I got into design without a degree. I used AI to build, but I kept running into the same problem.
Clients didn't understand my decisions, and I wasn't always sure I could explain them either. Ship69 was my answer — 69 days of building things that solve real problems, shipped every single day.

The goal: a portfolio strong enough to land a design engineering role. The rule: miss 3 days and you're out. I didn't miss 3.

Every project taught me something new — about design, about shipping, about what actually matters when you're building for real people.
